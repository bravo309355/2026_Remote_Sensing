EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
─────────────────
A temporary barrier lake formed in Matai'an valley during Typhoon Colo (Aug 2025).
Using Sentinel-2 change detection with SCL cloud masking, we mapped water presence
and validated against 60 field-corrected ground-truth points.
NDWI proved more sensitive than NDVI for barrier lake detection.
